About Konami

Konami is a Japanese entertainment company known for its gaming products, including video games, trading cards, and arcade games. Founded in 1969, Konami has become one of the most prominent names in the gaming industry, with popular franchises such as Metal Gear, Silent Hill, and Pro Evolution Soccer.

The company has also expanded into other areas, such as health and fitness products, casinos, and digital entertainment. With a focus on innovation and quality, Konami continues to be a leading force in the world of entertainment.

For more information, visit Konami's official website.
